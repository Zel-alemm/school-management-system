Learning Begins With Us

We, at Lumame secondary school offer supportive and inspirational environments for young enquiring minds to learn and grow with us. our passion for learning means we achieve more than outstanding result.We strive to build confident and creative thinkers and aim at delivering an education that is relevant to their future.

We are an early learning academy focused on social-emotional development and early literacy and numeracy. Our students walk out with the character and confidence to make their mark in the world, equipped with the knowledge an real world skills that take them way ahead in the industry they may serve.
